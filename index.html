My Experience with RPG Maker MZ

My experience with RPG Maker MZ has been very positive. The reason it's so positive is due to how easy and simple RPG Maker is to use, this lead to my experience being positive since it allowed me to focus on my creativity instead of focusing on how to get something to happen or work.
